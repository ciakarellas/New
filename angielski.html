​We derived knowledge and understanding of real estate market from long-term practical experience gained through the cooperation with a number of institutional and private investors, architectural offices, law firms, banks and real estate agencies.

We are active on the real estate market since 2003. We are entitled to conduct property valuations as a certified real estate valuator by Ministry of construction.

We are specializing on residential market (multi-family housing, single-family) and commercial real estates (office buildings, shopping centers, warehouses).

Only reliable and professional approach to the valuation guarantees to take appropriate investment decisions.
